CPE SOLAR

Chaitany Pinnacle Enterprises is a fast-growing solar energy solutions provider based in Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh. With two years of dedicated experience, we specialise in the sale and distribution of premium solar panels from leading brands such as Tata Power Solar, Waaree, Adani Solar, Vikram Solar, and Loom Solar. Under the leadership of CEO Mr. Ajit Singh, we are committed to promoting green sustainability and energy independence across urban and rural Uttar Pradesh. Registered under UPNEDA, we proudly serve domestic, commercial, and industrial clients from our base at Gulab Khera, Bijnor Road, Banthra, Lucknow-226401, empowering communities with reliable and clean solar solutions for a brighter future.
